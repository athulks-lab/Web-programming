College Of Engineering Cherthala



Dept. Of CS

The College of Engineering Cherthala offers Computer Science (CS) related programs, including a 4-year B.Tech in Computer Science
and Engineering (CSE) and a 2-year Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program, in addition to a B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and
Data Science. The department provides training in a broad curriculum covering modern hardware and software, ensuring graduates meet
global demands for high-caliber computer engineers.



Dept. Of EC

The College of Engineering Cherthala (CEC) offers a B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) . The Electronics
Engineering Department provides training in modern electronics with a focus on both foundational concepts and advanced applications.
